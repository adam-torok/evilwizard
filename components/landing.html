About the game

“Evil Wizard” is a humor-filled action RPG that reverts the role of the classic villain, making it the protagonist of the story as it rises from defeat to reclaim his castle from the heroes that sieged it. Fight in melee with the sword and cast spells using the staff. Gather elements from the environment like fire from torches, electricity from generators, and more, to combine them and discover new spells to defeat enemies and solve puzzles.

We want to give players the opportunity to explore a new perspective of the classic story “hero must defeat the evil wizard to save the world”. The game puts players in the role of the evil wizard, showing them what happened with him after the heroes successfully defeated him. How did he manage to rise once again and regain his power? What happened to the evil wizard after the credits rolled and the heroes saved the world again and again? This particular take on this thematic character gives us a lot of room to play with as we portray him as an anti hero with a fresh new look, telling the story from his point of view with jokes, funny cinematics and deep fast combat.

Features

Humor

Funny dialogues and in-game cinematics. The evil wizard has a deadpool vibe as he tells the story from his point of view

Elements

Solve puzzles and combat challenges by harnessing different elements from the environment. With five elements to choose from, explore different combinations to gain an advantage in battle.

Action

Defeat heroes by casting spells using the power of the elements, get them from the environment or use your sword for close combat encounters.

Who is the "Evil Wizard" ?

Professional final boss trying to revive his fading career after his last defeat in an attempt for a chance at a sequel, or at least a premium DLC

Recognized by many as a staple of evilness and power, by some as a deadly enemy, by your team as your next investment. With decades of experience this classic character dominates the hearts of millions, some as fans others as undead minions rising from their graves to fight under his command.

With a big, huge staff on his left hand, and a sword on his right hand he exterminates any hero that opposes him. Harnessing elements from the environment to cast powerful spells to sun, freeze, shock or poison his enemies before finishing them off with his sword, makes him a true master of the elements.
